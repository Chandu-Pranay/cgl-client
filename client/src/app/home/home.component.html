Comercial General Liability Insurance

In view of new dimensions of Legal Liability Exposures any Business can fall victim to the high cost of liability from simple slip and fall accidents to personal injury and reputation damage claims. Commercial General Liability (CGL) Policy covers third party liabilities arising from various business operations, be tt Premises, Products And Completed Operations and/or Advertising and Personal Injury.

What can be Covered...


Bodily Injury

The insurer pays off medical expenses for claims arising due to physical injuries,mental injuries,humilation and shock.

Legal Lawsuit

For claims arising due to the invasion of privacy, the insurer shall help you out wih the out-of-the court and legal settlement costs.

Property Damage

Includes premisis damages and also compensates for third-party property expenses such as replacement,repair and renovation costs.

Who needs Commercial General Liability Insurance

This policy is important for every small and medium sized businesses to protect the insured entity against claims arising out of legal liability where they are held responsible for third party bodily injury or property damage due to insured's business, premises or products. It should be taken by every new business as it covers all risk a business may face.
